Mission Statement

The goal of the Holistic Health Network is to build a co-creative community where people can come together to explore health, healing, and wellness. The network supports undergraduate students in the Holistic Health department with their studies by providing a living library, a safe space for project collaboration, and academic advising in HSS 329. Services
